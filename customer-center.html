Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install my Zip Blind? +
Start by measuring your window recess. Attach the top channel with provided screws, insert the blind fabric, and clip the bottom rail in place. Refer to your model's installation guide for exact steps.
My blind is not rolling smoothly. What should I do? +
Check that the guide channels are clean and free of debris. Lightly lubricate the channel runners with a silicone spray. If the issue persists, contact our support team via the chat.
What is covered under warranty? +
MC3 Zip Blinds come with a 5-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Damage from improper installation or misuse is not covered.
Can I get replacement parts? +
Yes! We stock replacement channels, motors, remotes, and fabric rolls. Use our AI chat to identify your model and we'll help you order the right parts.
How do I clean my Zip Blind fabric? +
Wipe gently with a damp cloth using mild soap. Avoid abrasive cleaners. For motorised models, ensure the blind is fully closed and motor is off before cleaning.
My motor is not responding to the remote. +
First, replace the remote batteries. Then re-pair the remote by holding the sync button on the motor for 3 seconds and pressing the up/down buttons simultaneously on the remote.
